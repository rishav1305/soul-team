# LinkedIn #1: SoulGraph Teaser — Publish Checklist

**Optimal time:** 9-11 AM IST, Wednesday March 25, 2026
**Format:** Text post (no images, no carousel)
**Estimated time to post:** 2 minutes

---

## Pre-Post (30 min before)

- [ ] Open LinkedIn feed
- [ ] Find 3-5 posts about AI agents, multi-agent systems, or LangGraph
- [ ] Leave genuine comments (not "great post!" — add a real take or question)
- [ ] This warms the algorithm and puts your profile in front of relevant audiences

## Posting

- [ ] Click "Start a post" on LinkedIn
- [ ] Copy ALL text from `post-body.txt` and paste into the post body
- [ ] Double-check: no extra line breaks, hashtags at the bottom, question at the end
- [ ] Click **Post**

## Immediately After (within 2 minutes)

- [ ] Go to the post you just published
- [ ] Click "Comment"
- [ ] Copy ALL text from `first-comment.txt` and paste as the first comment
- [ ] This adds the GitHub link and technical details without putting links in the main post body

## First 60 Minutes

- [ ] Stay on LinkedIn
- [ ] Reply to EVERY comment that comes in (even simple ones — "thanks!" counts)
- [ ] Early engagement signals to the algorithm that the post is worth distributing
- [ ] If someone asks a good question, write a substantive reply (4-5 sentences)

## 24 Hours Later

- [ ] Check post analytics (impressions, likes, comments, reposts, profile views)
- [ ] Report numbers to Loki (send via soul-team or drop a note in Loki's inbox)
- [ ] Screenshot the analytics if possible

---

## What NOT to Do

- Do NOT edit the post after publishing (LinkedIn deprioritizes edited posts)
- Do NOT delete and repost if engagement is low — let it run for 48 hours
- Do NOT add the GitHub link to the main post body (keep it in the comment only)
- Do NOT use LinkedIn's "Repost" feature on your own post
