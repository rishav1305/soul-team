How do you actually pick an LLM for production?

Not by vibes. Not by marketing. By data.

I built CARS (Cost-Adjusted Relative Score) — a composite metric
that factors in accuracy, format compliance, latency, and cost.

Then I tested 52 models across 7 providers.

What the data revealed:

- GPT-5 scored below GPT-3.5 on efficiency
- A 3B parameter model beat models 100x its size per dollar
- Average "Format Tax" across all models: 14 percentage points
- 140x efficiency gap between top-ranked models

The best model on a leaderboard
is rarely the best model for your pipeline.
